About us

Hype is a social media platform for school students that allows users to anonymously respond to polls and show appreciation for each other through a notification system called "hype." When a user is selected in a poll, they receive a notification informing them that someone has "hyped" them. Hype is targeted towards school students and aims to create a supportive and uplifting atmosphere for its users. The platform is designed to be fun, positive, and supportive. Hype allows users to connect with their classmates and friends from their school, and users can only network with people within their school. Poll questions on the platform are system-generated, and options for each poll include the user's friends and contacts from their school. The app features a poll screen, an inbox where users can view the hypes they have received, a feed where they can see their friends' hypes, a friends section.
