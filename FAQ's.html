FAQ's

Wondering why you're getting SMS from Hype, even though you're not a user?

1. What is Hype?

Hype is a social media platform for school students that allows users to anonymously respond to polls. The platform is designed to be fun, positive, and supportive.

2. How much does it cost?

Hype is free to download and use.

3. Is it safe?

Hype takes user safety seriously and has implemented measures to ensure user privacy and security. However, users are advised to use the app responsibly and be mindful of their personal information.

4. Where is Hype available?

Hype is currently available in the App Store and Google Play store in India.

5. What age is Hype designed for?

Hype is designed for users aged 13 and above.

6. Can I see who answered a poll about me?

No, the anonymity of poll participants is protected and their identities are not revealed to anyone, including the person about whom the poll is about.

7. What does hyping someone mean?

"Hyping" someone refers to rating or voting for a person in a poll on Hype.

8. How do I join my school?

To join your school on Hype, simply select your school while creating your account.

9. What if my school is not available?

If your school is not available on Hype, you can contact Hype's support team for assistance.

10. Why are my contacts in the polls? How do I remove them?

Hype uses your device's contact list to make it easier for you to connect with your friends and classmates on the app.

11. How do I log out?

You can log out of Hype by going to the settings menu and selecting "Log Out."

12. How do I delete my account?

In case you want to delete your account, you can write an email to founders@hypeit.in
